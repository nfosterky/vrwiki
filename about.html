VR Wiki is a virtual reality reader which loads content from wikipedia.com. It is made for smartphones which can be used with Google Cardboard.

To use, simply type in what you want to learn about then look around for the page. In the center of the display is a pointer, which can be used to interact with the page. To open new pages simply rest the pointer over a link. Scroll up and down by looking at the top and bottom of the pages.

For Android phones you can use the camera to see the outside world while reading the content. The camera is not required to use the app.
